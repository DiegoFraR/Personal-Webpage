    About Me

    Learning the World of Software Engineering

    While in high school, I decided to try out an intro to programming course. Little did I know that this little thing we call coding would turn into one of my passions. I began by writing simple one-off Java programs such as FizzBuzz and things similar to it. I enjoyed the unique challenge that coding provided. It allowed me to express my strong mathematical skills and creativity. While researching for undergraduate degrees to pursue, I noticed the software engineering degree that Kennesaw State University offered and instantly knew this is what I wanted to study. I love working on big picture projects, and software engineering seemed to align perfectly.

    Current Goals

    My current goals are to continue learning new things about the Software Engineering industry and finish my studies at Kennesaw State University. My anticipated graduation date is May 2025, and I am excited to start working as a Software Engineer in the industry. In my free time, I enjoy developing side projects as these allow me to learn about new technologies that my university does not teach. I learn best through hands-on learning, and these side projects provide an outlet for me to learn and be creative.

    Hobbies

    Whenever I am not at my computer, I enjoy spending time with my family, exercising, playing sports (soccer mainly), and learning about other things in the world. The latter tends to send me down a rabbit hole 99% of the time.
